Empowering Armenia with AI4ALL

A nationwide initiative by EIF to equip Armenian youth, educators, and professionals with transformative AI skills.

What is AI4ALL?

Launched by the Enterprise Incubator Foundation, AI4ALL is a multi-city initiative designed to democratize AI education in Armenia. It includes hands-on workshops, industry talks, and real-world projects for students aged 14–24.

Programs are held in Gyumri, Yerevan, Vanadzor, and other regions to make AI accessible and inclusive—especially for youth in underserved communities.
