Artificial Intelligence for Everyone

"AI4ALL empowers young minds and educators to explore the possibilities of AI — equipping them with tools to shape the future."

Our Mission

AI4ALL by EIF is dedicated to transforming technology education in Armenia by making Artificial Intelligence accessible and inclusive. We help youth, educators, and professionals develop practical AI skills that match labor-market demands and inspire innovation.

Through workshops, competitions, and community engagement, AI4ALL nurtures future-ready talent, bridging regional gaps and fostering an inclusive tech ecosystem.

Programs & Workshops

Programming with AI (Gyumri, Shirak)

A 1‑month in-person workshop teaching youth to apply AI tools in software development. About 100 Shirak-region students joined the first round (Nov 2024) and ~70 in the second (Jan 2025).

Participants build practical coding projects using AI, aligning skills with labor-market needs. Instructor: Regional tech experts.

“I wanted to better understand how to apply AI more efficiently in my work and expect to be familiar with more AI tools tailored to programming—tools that will help me complete tasks faster and more effectively.” – Samvel Manasyan

Graphic Design with AI (Gyumri, Shirak)

A parallel 1‑month workshop on using AI in digital design. The same cohorts of ~100 then ~70 youth (mostly artists and designers) learned to incorporate AI into their creative workflows. Instructor: Industry artists/educators.

“I was thrilled to explore how I can use AI to enhance my creative process, aiming to complete an entire portfolio of works using AI tools by the end of the course.” – Knarik Harutyunyan

Video Creation & Editing with AI (Gyumri, Shirak)

A 1‑month offline workshop launched Dec 2024 where youth learn AI tools for video production and editing.

“I gained valuable knowledge and skills on how to apply AI in video production… interested in both technical aspects and content development techniques that lead to high-quality videos.” – Arpine Arakelyan

Creative Coding with AI (S.A. Lab, Azatan, Syunik)

A free 1‑month course for 14–18-year-olds introducing programming fundamentals through AI. Launched Spring 2025 at Sergey Aslanyan Lab. Instructor: Christina Martirosyan.

Students gain foundational programming skills and apply AI tools to develop their own interactive digital projects.

General AI Fundamentals (Online webinars)

Online “Artificial Intelligence Basics” series for broad audiences, emphasizing AI philosophy, ethics, prompts, and generative AI tools. Over 400 participants joined these free webinars.

Instructor: Grisha Hovsepyan. The program spun off an AI Club under EIF’s Wave youth initiative to foster peer learning.

“AI4ALL – Code with Vibes” Competition & Workshops (Nationwide)

Summer 2025 tech contest for students and young innovators to build AI-powered projects. Top prizes: ₷400,000, ₷200,000, and ₷100,000. Winners get free access to Engineering City’s high-tech accelerator space.

Preparatory online workshops by AI expert Avetik Mkhitaryan (July 17–19) covered web dev, prompt engineering, and AI-powered projects.

Participants & Impact

Reach & Engagement

  • 500+ youths (ages 14–18) participated in Shirak and Syunik region courses during 2024–25.
  • 400+ participants joined online AI webinar rounds.
  • Courses are open to all genders; low-cost or free participation.
  • 1000+ youth engaged through EIF initiatives including Wave youth clubs.
  • Programs held regionally to broaden tech access beyond Yerevan.

Collaborations & Strategy

AI4ALL is run by the Enterprise Incubator Foundation (EIF) under USAID’s Armenia Workforce Development Activity, partnering with Gyumri Technology Center, Sergey Aslanyan Foundation, Engineering City, and others.

These partners provide venues, expert instructors, mentorship, and co-working spaces. This ecosystem helps youth gain hands-on AI skills and connect to the tech labor market.

The program complements university curricula and vocational programs by focusing on practical AI applications and inclusivity — especially supporting girls and rural students.

Competition winners receive accelerator space and funding to jumpstart their careers, reinforcing Armenia’s growing tech innovation ecosystem.
