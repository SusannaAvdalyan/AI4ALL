About AI4ALL

A bold movement to ensure that every Armenian youth has access to world-class AI education.

Our Story & Mission

Founded in 2024, AI4ALL Armenia is a national initiative by the Enterprise Incubator Foundation and USAID. Our mission: to democratize AI knowledge, foster innovation, and build tech talent in every region of Armenia.

  • Workshops in Gyumri, Vanadzor, Yerevan, and beyond
  • Hands-on mentorship from tech professionals
  • Over 400 students engaged in AI webinars

About the Enterprise Incubator Foundation (EIF)

The Enterprise Incubator Foundation is Armenia’s leading technology NGO dedicated to fostering innovation and supporting the country’s digital transformation. EIF drives impactful initiatives, including AI4ALL, to empower youth and professionals with skills to thrive in the digital economy.
