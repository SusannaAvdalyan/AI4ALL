About AI4ALL

A bold movement to ensure that every Armenian youth has access to world-class AI education.

Our Story & Mission

Launched in 2024, AI4ALL Armenia is a nationwide initiative by the Enterprise Incubator Foundation and USAID to bring AI literacy and opportunity to every region in Armenia.

  • On-the-ground workshops bringing AI to life — from Yerevan to the most remote villages
  • Future-ready skills built through real projects, not just lectures
  • 1,000+ students and educators empowered with cutting-edge AI training — and counting

About the Enterprise Incubator Foundation (EIF)

EIF is Armenia's leading tech NGO, fostering digital transformation through innovation, education, and entrepreneurship. From launching national tech parks to nurturing the next generation of AI leaders, EIF is building Armenia's digital future.
