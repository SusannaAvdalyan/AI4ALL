Empowering Armenia with AI4ALL

A national initiative by the Enterprise Incubator Foundation to make AI education accessible, equitable, and empowering for Armenia's youth—wherever they live.

What is AI4ALL?

AI4ALL educational initiative aims to equip people from various professions and interests with the knowledge and skills needed to effectively use AI tools and be prepared for upcoming changes. The initiative’s courses and workshops are conducted in Armenian, providing participants with modern and relevant content.

Our mission is to ensure that every student—from Yerevan to Syunik—can access the tools, mentors, and knowledge needed to innovate with AI.
