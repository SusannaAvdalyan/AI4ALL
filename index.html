Empowering Armenia with AI4ALL

A national initiative by the Enterprise Incubator Foundation to make AI education accessible, equitable, and empowering for Armenia’s youth—wherever they live.

What is AI4ALL?

AI4ALL is a national ecosystem for AI literacy in Armenia, launched by EIF in collaboration with strategic partners. Through immersive bootcamps, school integration, and open-access content, we aim to close the digital divide and prepare youth (ages 14–24) for careers in the global AI economy.

Our mission is to ensure that every student—from Yerevan to Syunik—can access the tools, mentors, and knowledge needed to innovate with AI.
